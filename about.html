About Us


Founded by Miss Rabiatul Adawiyah, Scentify Hub came to life through a remarkable passion for perfumes. It all began with Rabiatul's fascination with the captivating power of scents and her desire to share this enchantment with others. Inspired by her own experiences and driven by a deep appreciation for fragrance craftsmanship, she embarked on a journey to create a haven for perfume lovers. With unwavering dedication, Rabiatul meticulously curated a collection of exquisite perfumes, handpicking each scent for its unique character and allure. Scentify Hub emerged as a testament to Rabiatul's commitment to offering an exceptional olfactory experience, providing a gateway to a world of captivating fragrances that speak to the heart and soul of every individual who encounters them.





Vision



To become the premier destination for online perfume purchases, offering a wide collection of brands, a great shopping experience, and high customer satisfaction.






Mission



To provide customers with a seamless online shopping experience for purchasing their favorite perfumes, offering a wide selection of high-quality brands, exceptional customer service, and personalized fragrance recommendations.








Objective



Increase User Engagement and Website Traffic
Increase Brand Diversity and Partnerships




Enhance Customer Satisfaction and Loyalty
